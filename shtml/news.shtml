Other News

We are delighted to welcome Assistant Professor of Psychology Suzy Scherf to the Cognitive and Developmental Areas. Scherf is a developmental cognitive neuroscientist with interests in visual cognition, especially face processing and object recognition, in both normal children and those with autism spectrum disorder.
