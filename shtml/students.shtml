Information For Prospective Students

Prospective graduate students are encouraged to learn more about the Department and our faculty's research interests. Most faculty welcome email inquiries from prospective students. In any given year, we admit 3-5 graduate students from among a pool of 40-50 applications, so admissions are competitive. The application deadline for Fall admission is December 1. The faculty will invite a subset of students to visit Penn State during our graduate student visiting weekend in February.
