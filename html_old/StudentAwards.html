We are particularly proud of the many achievements of graduate students in our program. Many of our graduate students have been recognized by receiving highly competitive awards both within and outside the University. Some recent highlights include:

Marios Avraamides: Avraamides, M., & Ritter, F. E. (2002). Using multidisciplinary expert evaluations to test and improve cognitive model interfaces. In Proceedings of the 11th Computer Generated Forces Conference. 553-562, 02-CGF-100. Orlando, FL: U. of Central Florida. - One of only seven papers selected by the Conference Program Committee for the Recommended Reading List from the 11th Conference on Computer-Generated Forces and Behavior Representation. www.sisostds.org/conference/view_public_reading.cfm?Phase_ID=2
