FACULTY NAMED APA FELLOW

Michael Wenger has been elected as a Fellow in the American Psychological Association (Division 3 - Experimental), in recognition of the unusual and outstanding contributions he has made to research and the advancement of knowledge in his field. He joins Rich Carlson, Judith Kroll, Cathleen Moore, and David Rosenbaum as Fellows of Division 3.
